Why Does MAGFest Need My Legal Name?

To help ensure the safety of everyone at MAGFest, registrations are tied to attendees using their photo ID. Attendees present their photo ID upon check-in to claim their badge.

If you are unsure what name to use, or if your legal name is in flux, simply enter the name that will appear on your photo ID when you check in.

Your legal name will not appear on your badge.
